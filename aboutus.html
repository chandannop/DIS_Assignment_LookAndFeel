About Me

My name is Sai Chandan Prabhakara Reddy, and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Business Analytics and Information Systems at the University of South Florida. I am passionate about leveraging data analytics to drive business decisions and enhance operational efficiency.

I work as a Research Assistant at the Center for Urban Transportation Research (CUTR) at USF, where I am involved in cutting-edge research projects focused on improving urban mobility. Prior to this, I worked at Samsung R&D, where I contributed to innovative technology solutions.

This website is an example of my work, combining my interests in web development and cycling. Through this project, I aim to provide valuable information and resources for bicycle enthusiasts.

Development Process

This website was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. The main objective was to create an interactive and responsive site that provides useful information about bicycles. Throughout the development process, I focused on ensuring a seamless user experience and incorporating modern web design principles.

The development process involved extensive planning and research, followed by iterative design and development phases. Some key aspects of the development process included:

  • Wireframing and prototyping to visualize the layout and functionality.
  • Utilizing modern web technologies and frameworks to enhance performance and interactivity.
  • Conducting thorough testing to identify and fix bugs, ensuring a smooth user experience.
